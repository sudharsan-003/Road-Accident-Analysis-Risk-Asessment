# 🚦 Road Accident Analysis and Risk Assessment Dashboard (2022–2025)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive dashboard designed to analyze road accidents across major Indian cities between 2022 and 2025. It focuses on understanding accident trends, severity, and key contributing risk factors such as traffic conditions, weather, and time patterns.

The dashboard enables users to explore accident data dynamically and gain actionable insights that can support road safety improvements and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Road accidents are a major public safety concern, leading to significant loss of life and property. However, understanding the patterns and contributing factors behind these accidents is often challenging due to complex and multi-dimensional data.

This project aims to:

- Identify accident trends over time
- Analyze severity and casualty patterns
- Understand key risk factors such as weather, traffic density, and peak hours
- Highlight high-risk conditions and scenarios

---

## 📊 Dataset

- **Source:** Kaggle
- **Size:** ~20,000 rows
- **Description:**
  The dataset includes detailed information on road accidents such as:
  - Accident severity (Minor, Major, Fatal)
  - Number of vehicles involved
  - Casualties
  - City and location data
  - Time-based features
  - Environmental conditions
  - Road and traffic conditions (road type, traffic density)
  - Risk Score (pre-engineered feature representing accident risk level)
